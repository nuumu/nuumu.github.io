Musicians

A roundup of world-class musicians


ADIOS

This talented and ambitious band has caught the world in a frenzy with their current album, Wanna get away. With more than one million copies sold and being number one in the Top 10 playlist for 14 weeks, the trio—vocalist and guitarist Sarah Sterwicks, bassist Bryan Corts, and drummer Shane Grundin—has sold out on over 100 national and regional tours this year.

Elephant in the Room

Since last year's debut, Elephant in the Room has risen in popularity and continues to garner the attention and interest of several audiences from across the world. Vocalist Wyatt Andros, guitarists Davy Freize and Arnold Schvartz, bassist Jim Thomas, and drummer Tony Ciarra have proved their skill and worth in the music industry with their first album, White Out!, which sold over 500,000 copies last year. Finding themselves more in demand with an ever-growing list of loyal followers, Elephant in the Room is hitting the roads and touring worldwide to deliver what fans want most—the best Epic music experience.

Jacob Reefer

"Music is my passion. I come from a family of musicians, so it's literally in my blood." Jacob's passion is ever-present as his unique style of vocalism has caught the rapt attention of every listener. Melding melodies with heavy and hard beats, the stylistic and eclectic songs he creates enthralls listeners like a Pied Piper. His highly acclaimed singles, Rock City  and You're my favorite,  have been voted as the Top 10 Best Songs of 2016 by National Music Awards.

Trisha Simms

Trisha Simms, also known as Trish-Shims on stage, is one of the youngest headliner known for her charismatic dance performances and powerful vocals. She has captured the new generation with her fiery spirit, winning enthusiasm, and catchy songs. Trisha holds three record-hitting songs that were ranked number one on several playlists. She is releasing her sixth album this year.
